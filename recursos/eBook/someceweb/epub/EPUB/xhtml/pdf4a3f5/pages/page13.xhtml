Julio C. Sosa received the Engineering degree in Electronic Engineering in 1997 from
ITLAC, Mich. Mexico. The M. Sc. in Electrical Engineering, in 2000 from
CINVESTAV-IPN, Mexico and the PhD in Technology of Information,
Communication and Computation in 2007 from the University of Valencia, Spain.
Currently he is Titular Professor at CICATA-Qro. Instituto Politécnico Nacional,
Mexico. His current research interests are signal and image processing, sensor
networks and embedded systems.
